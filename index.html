About Me

My name is Zachary, but I prefer to go by Zach. I am a graduate of Blacksburg High School's class of '17 in Blacksburg, Virginia, and I will be attending Virginia Tech as an Aerospace Engineer in the class of '21.

My favorite hobbies are exercising, hanging out with friends, snowboarding, paint-balling, and programming. I started learning how to program back in middle school, when I taught myself JavaScript and Shell. I eventually learned Java when I started making Minecraft mods, and my programming expertise has just grown from there. I'm an alumni the FRC Team 401 here in Virginia, where I was head programmer. In the past two seasons, we made it to the world championships in St. Louis, MO, and won the Engineering Inspiration award at the district and regional level for our outreach in the Montgomery County area.

Please feel free to browse my site, which contains information of projects I have worked on and organizations I have worked with.
